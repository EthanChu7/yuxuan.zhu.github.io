This work addresses GNN explanation by jointly modeling necessity and sufficiency. It proposes NSEG, which optimizes a lower bound of probability of necessity and sufficiency (PNS) under a structural causal model with counterfactual estimation. Experiments show consistent gains over prior explainers in producing necessary and sufficient explanations.
