This paper argues that industrial ranking systems should adopt both context engineering and multi-step reasoning, not just Transformer architecture changes. It proposes OnePiece, a unified framework for retrieval and ranking with structured context engineering, block-wise latent reasoning, and progressive multi-task training. Deployed in Shopee personalized search, it reports consistent online gains including GMV/UU and ad revenue improvements.
