This work studies latent multi-step reasoning for sequential recommendation and identifies latent drift when reasoning is unconstrained. It proposes ManCAR, which constrains reasoning on a collaborative manifold using local intent priors and adaptive test-time stopping based on predictive stabilization. Benchmarks show consistent improvements over state-of-the-art methods.
