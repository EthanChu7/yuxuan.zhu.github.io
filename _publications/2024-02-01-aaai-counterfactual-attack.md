Deep neural networks (DNNs) are vulnerable to adversarial examples, but most methods ignore the causal generation process of data. This work analyzes vulnerability through causality and proposes CADE, a framework for generating counterfactual adversarial examples with realistic intervention effects. Experiments show strong performance across white-box, transfer-based, and random intervention attacks.
