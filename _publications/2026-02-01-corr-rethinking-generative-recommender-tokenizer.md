This paper revisits semantic-ID recommendation and proposes ReSID, a recommendation-native tokenization framework. ReSID combines field-aware masked auto-encoding for predictive item representation and globally aligned orthogonal quantization for compact, predictable SID sequences. Experiments across multiple datasets show consistent improvements over strong baselines with substantial tokenization cost reduction.
