This work targets temporal distribution shift in industrial recommenders and proposes ELBO_TDS, a probabilistic framework for incremental learning pipelines. It combines augmentation over time-varying factors with a causal-graph-grounded self-supervised variational objective to improve temporal generalization while avoiding representation collapse. It reports strong gains in production, including GMV per user uplift in Shopee Product Search.
