This paper studies feature attribution methods and introduces FANS, which measures feature importance via probability of necessity and sufficiency (PNS) in a local neighborhood. It uses a dual-stage perturbation test with counterfactual reasoning and resampling-based conditional estimation. Results across benchmarks show improved attribution quality over prior methods.
