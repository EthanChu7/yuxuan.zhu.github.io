This paper studies multi-feedback collaborative filtering and highlights limitations of feedback-wise disjoint-space modeling. It proposes generalized neural ordinal logistic regression (GNOLR), which learns structured dependencies in a unified embedding space with nested optimization. Experiments across real-world datasets show improved effectiveness, efficiency, and adaptability.
